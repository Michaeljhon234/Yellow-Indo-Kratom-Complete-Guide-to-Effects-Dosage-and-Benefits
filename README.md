**Signs of excessive dosing:**
- Severe nausea or vomiting
- Significant dizziness

**Important safety guidelines:**
- Never mix with alcohol or other depressants
- Avoid driving within 4-6 hours of dosing
- Stay hydrated (kratom can be dehydrating)
- Don't use if pregnant or breastfeeding
- Consult healthcare providers about medication interactions

**Common mistake**: Ignoring early tolerance signs and increasing doses instead of taking breaks. Better option is cycling off kratom for several days when effects diminish.

**Worth understanding about dependency:**
Yellow Indo can be habit-forming with daily use. Withdrawal symptoms are typically mild but can include irritability, restlessness, and sleep difficulties. The main point is that occasional use (2-3 times per week) significantly reduces dependency risk.

**When to avoid yellow Indo kratom:**

- Liver problems or disease
- Taking medications that affect liver enzymes
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding
